Tartan Details - Loch Lomond Whisky

The information held within The Scottish Register of Tartans for the "Loch Lomond Whisky" tartan is shown below.

Tartan date: 17/12/2024
Registration date: 23 December 2024
Category: Corporate
Restrictions: Yes. Exclusively designed by Kinloch Anderson for Loch Lomond Whisky. Restricted availability, please contact Kinloch Anderson regarding use.

Registration notes: This company tartan was designed exclusively for Loch Lomond Whisky.
